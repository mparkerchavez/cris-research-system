## Current Contents

### Talking Points
**Location:** `/Talking_Points/`

Weekly conversation starters for interviews, networking, and client conversations. Each talking point includes:
- **The Point** - 2-3 sentence conversation starter
- **Why It Matters** - Significance, implications, opportunities
- **Who Cares** - Relevant audiences (hiring managers, clients, investors, etc.)
- **Evidence** - Supporting sources from weekly synthesis
- **Your Angle** - Personal connection and positioning

**Latest:** [2026-02-01](Talking_Points/2026-02-01.md) (February 1-3, 2026)

## How to Use

1. **Before interviews/calls:** Review relevant Talking Points for conversation starters
2. **For client proposals:** Use briefs and one-pagers to demonstrate market knowledge
3. **For presentations:** Draw from pitch deck materials and case studies
4. **For content creation:** Reference talking points for blog posts, LinkedIn updates, etc.
