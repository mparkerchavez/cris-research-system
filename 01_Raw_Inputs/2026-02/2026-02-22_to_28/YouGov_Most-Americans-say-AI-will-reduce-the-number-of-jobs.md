# Most Americans say AI will reduce the number of jobs in the U.S.

## Metadata
* **Publisher:** YouGov
* **Published:** 2026-02-17
* **Type:** Article

* **Captured:** 2026-02-27

---

Majorities of Americans don't have much trust in AI and think companies are investing too much in it. Most Americans say AI will decrease the availability of jobs. More say it will have a negative effect on the economy than say it will have a positive effect. Most Americans have used artificial intelligence (AI), but only one-quarter use it regularly.

What you need to know about Americans' views on artificial intelligence, as of [the February 13 - 16, 2026 Economist / YouGov Poll](https://d3nkl3psvxxpe9.cloudfront.net/documents/econTabReport_usZL1Jt.pdf#page=66):

- About two-thirds (63%) of Americans have used AI but only about one-quarter (23%) use it regularly
  - Younger adults are more likely than older Americans to have used AI

- Americans are more likely to say AI will have a negative overall effect on the economy than to say its effect will be mostly positive (45% vs. 16%). One-quarter (24%) say its effect will be equally positive and negative
  - Younger adults and Republicans are more likely to say AI will have an overall positive effect than older Americans, Democrats, and Independents. But in all groups, a larger share say the effect will be mostly negative
