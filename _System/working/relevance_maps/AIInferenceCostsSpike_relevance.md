This source establishes the **infrastructure layer crisis** as a critical driver of 2026 bifurcation. The exponential demand growth (10x annually) paired with absolute supply constraints (2028+) creates a structural constraint that will:

1. **Eliminate pure play SaaS pricing models** (DP15: Notion losing 10 margin points)
2. **Force organizational redesign** from ownership to managed service architecture (DP22: 2-year depreciation cycles)
3. **Elevate design/architecture competency** as primary differentiator (DP21, DP23: routing layers and efficiency investments)
4. **Create governance discontinuity** requiring real-time observability and rapid allocation (DP5, DP20, DP24)
5. **Bifurcate trust models** based on hyperscaler relationship access (DP3, DP16, DP17)

The 6-month action window (DP24) validates that 2026 is decision point, not observation point. Organizations must secure capacity/design routing layers/adopt efficiency practices NOW or face cost/capacity shock by mid-2026.
