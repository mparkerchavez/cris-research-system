## 5. Tensions / Contradictions to Explore

### Tension 1: Scale vs. Simplicity
- **DP6 + DP7:** Single developer manages 30k daily transcriptions; 20 FTE manage 4 products
- **Conflict:** If 1 person + AI can handle production at significant scale, why does Every still need 20 FTE?
- **Hypothesis:** Non-engineering work (sales, operations, finance) + business strategy still requires headcount
- **Research Gap:** What's the overhead distribution across product, operations, business?

### Tension 2: Autonomy vs. Consistency
- **DP8:** Two-slice teams gain autonomy and "move extremely quickly on new opportunities"
- **DP4:** Success requires flexible reallocation of design/growth/marketing across products
- **Conflict:** How do organizations maintain product consistency and brand coherence when teams pivot weekly?
- **Implication:** Consistency governance becomes critical in AI-native orgs

### Tension 3: Skill Portability Paradox
- **DP5:** AI literacy enables rapid context transfer
- **DP8:** Success requires "general managers" with broad autonomy
- **Conflict:** If AI handles context transfer, why do humans need to be "general managers"? What domain knowledge persists?
- **Research Gap:** Which decisions require human judgment vs. AI + AI literacy?
