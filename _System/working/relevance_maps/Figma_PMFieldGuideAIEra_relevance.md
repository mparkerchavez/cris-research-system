## SECTION 3: STRATEGIC GAPS & OMISSIONS

### Absent or Underexplored

1. **Tool-Coworker Duality (AI1):** Guide treats AI tools as productivity enablers, not as worker-class agents requiring management. No discussion of AI as peer decision-maker or delegation target.

2. **Coordination Tax (AI2):** Mentions "blurred boundaries" but doesn't discuss cost of increased cross-functional overhead or when coordination fails to create value.

3. **Implementation Chasm (AI3):** No discussion of gap between prototype and production; assumes seamless scale-up.

4. **Trust Multiplier (AI9):** Documents need for stakeholder understanding (DP17) but doesn't address trust deficit or verification mechanisms for AI-generated outputs.

5. **Context Quality (AI11):** Mentions context fragmentation (DP7) but treats as pure consolidation problem, not as quality/staleness/accuracy issue affecting agent performance.

6. **Cost Accessibility Stratification (AI16):** No discussion of tooling cost implications or how access to AI amplifies PM advantage unevenly across organizations.
