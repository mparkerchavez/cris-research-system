## INITIAL OBSERVATIONS

This Sequoia Capital essay marks a definitional inflection point for the investment and startup communities: AGI capability has transitioned from theoretical construct to operational reality through the convergence of three technical ingredients (pre-training, inference-time reasoning, and long-horizon agentic iteration). The authors deliberately adopt a functional rather than technical definition, positioning AGI through the lens of market impact and business outcomes rather than philosophical completeness—a rhetorical move that validates investor pragmatism while acknowledging definitional ambiguity persists among researchers. The 31-minute recruiter agent case study exemplifies the qualitative shift from constrained task automation to unconstrained problem-solving: agents now navigate ambiguity, form and test hypotheses, recognize dead ends, and pivot autonomously without scripted workflows, fundamentally altering the value proposition of AI applications.

The document identifies a critical market inflection from "talkers" (2023-2024 conversational interfaces) to "doers" (2026-2027 autonomous agents), with cascading implications for work organization, pricing models, and organizational structures. The exponential acceleration curve—doubabilities doubling every seven months—positions day-scale competency by 2028 and year-scale by 2034, establishing a timeline consensus among major institutional investors and researchers (METR tracking). This represents entry-level hiring disruption potential and workforce transformation pressure: workers transition from execution roles to management of parallel agent teams, fundamentally restructuring organizational hierarchy and capability requirements.

Operationally, the text emphasizes two complementary technical approaches: research institutions advancing reinforcement learning (intrinsic focus maintenance) while application-layer companies engineer robust agent harnesses (scaffolding, memory management, compaction). The asymmetric time compression—31-minute completion of multi-week recruiting workflows, century-scale comprehensiveness enabling clinical trial cross-referencing and tax code refactoring—signals competitive disruption intensity. However, the authors acknowledge persistent failure modes (hallucination, context loss, confident errors) while asserting improving addressability through harness refinement and feedback loops, maintaining cautious optimism about trajectory without overstating current reliability.
