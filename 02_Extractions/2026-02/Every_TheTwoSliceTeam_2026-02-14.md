## Initial Observations

This article documents a fundamental organizational shift enabled by advanced coding models. Every's operational model demonstrates that single-person teams with AI support can manage production complexity previously requiring multi-person teams. The key innovation is not just code generation but the extension of AI assistance across customer service, business strategy, and marketing decision-making. The external support model (design/growth/marketing as flexible agencies) reveals how organizations might restructure beyond engineering roles. This challenges traditional headcount-to-complexity scaling assumptions and suggests workforce architecture optimization is a critical strategic lever for AI-native companies.
