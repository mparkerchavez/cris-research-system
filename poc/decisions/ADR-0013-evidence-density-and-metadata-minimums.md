# ADR-0013: Evidence-density and metadata minimums for synthesis quality

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-02-09
- Confidence: inferred
- Owners: CRIS synthesis + pulse UX

## Context

Historical update notes indicate that shallow citation density and missing trend metadata reduced trust and utility in Pulse-facing synthesis outputs.

## Decision

Adopt quality minimums in synthesis templates/protocol updates:

- multi-source evidence density per insight/takeaway
- explicit tag-level metadata sections (for example key developments, idea connections)
- quality checklists before finalization

## Consequences

- Positive:
  - richer, more traceable synthesis and better Pulse display quality
  - stronger cross-source convergence signals
- Negative / Tradeoffs:
  - increased writing effort and stricter template compliance
