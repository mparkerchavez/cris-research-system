# ADR-0009: Phased weekly synthesis with sub-agent extraction mining

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-02-07
- Confidence: confirmed
- Owners: CRIS synthesis workflow

## Context

Single-pass weekly synthesis missed evidence because extraction mining and narrative writing competed for limited context.

## Decision

Use a phased workflow:

1. extraction mining (often via parallel sub-agents)
2. coverage sweep
3. Weekly Learnings writing from relevance maps
4. Active Ideas + Current Synthesis updates
5. Talking Points generation

## Consequences

- Positive:
  - higher evidence coverage and less omission risk
  - clearer separation of analysis vs narrative tasks
- Negative / Tradeoffs:
  - operational overhead across multiple phases/sessions
